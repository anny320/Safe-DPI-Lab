What is SafeDPI Simulator?

You'll step into a real decision-making role and build a national digital system from the ground up. Every choice you make — on identity verification, data storage, procurement, and oversight — shifts your Trust, Inclusion, Privacy, Security, and Governance scores. The goal isn't to get every score to 100%; it's to see the real trade-offs that Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) leaders face, and to walk away with a personalized Safeguards Report you can share and learn from.
