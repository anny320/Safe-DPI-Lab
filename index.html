Why this exists

Governments worldwide are racing to launch Digital ID, payments, and health platforms — but speed often comes at the cost of privacy, inclusion, and public trust. SafeDPI Simulator turns the UN Digital Public Infrastructure Safeguards Framework into a hands-on experience: every decision you make has a visible, immediate consequence, so you can feel — not just read about — the trade-offs of building digital infrastructure responsibly.
